About Me

Xavier

I'm Xavier Goh and will be graduating with a Higher Nitec in Cyber Security & Network from the Institute of Technical Education.

I am a driven, motivated learner and a team-player always looking for ways to improve myself and expand my skill set.

Over the past 3 years, I have had opportunities to learn various technical skills that have developed me as an individual. These skills include: Managing computer networks and end-user computer systems and devices, and programming.

I have also worked on a network that transfers information and data in between switches and routers using an Access Control List, VLAN inter-routing, and router-on-a-stick testing using a Cisco Packet tracer have broadened my knowledge in coding.

In my free time, I also like to read up and stay updated with the latest trends in IT, and plan to take up professional certifications in cybersecurity in the future.

In the future, I hope to work in the field of Cybersecurity Operations or Digital Forensics. My early interest in IT has been shaped by my father, who is an IT Security Officer securing our country’s private data. Over time, this interest has grown into a calling to pursue a career where I can protect systems, investigate threats and make a difference in safeguarding Singapore digital infrastructure.
